The OG Pass is an exclusive membership NFT that unlocks premium benefits within TheVapeLabs ecosystem. It grants holders profit-sharing, free vape devices, staking APY boosts, and early access to sales and events.
What benefits do OG Pass holders get?
OG Pass holders receive:
  • Free Omega Series Smart Vape Device
  • Free TheVapeNads NFT
  • 6 Free Pods
  • 15% lifetime discount on all TheVapeLabs products & services
